Who we are

Built on the ground, not just on paper.

🪪 At a Glance

  • Founded: 23 September 2020
  • Founder: Mr. Govind Shukla
  • Structure: Section 8 Non-Profit
  • Base: Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh
  • Reach: 28 states across India

In a world that often feels divided, InAmigos Foundation works toward unity and dignity for every community it touches. What began as a small, local effort in Chhattisgarh has grown into a network of volunteers and partners running programmes across education, nutrition, women's livelihoods, animal welfare, environmental care, and youth skilling.

We don't chase scale for its own sake — every initiative is designed to close a specific gap: a child without a classroom, a family without a meal, a woman without a steady income, a street animal without care, a neighbourhood without a tree cover. That's the InAmigos way — programmatic, local, and accountable.

Where the work happens

Six projects. One mission.

Every initiative targets a distinct need — together they form a complete cycle of care, from a child's first classroom to a family's first steady income.

Nutrition

Project Sewa सेवा

Distributing food and clothing to underprivileged families, making sure no one in our reach sleeps hungry or unclothed.

Education

Project Bachpanshala बचपनशाला

Bringing quality, joyful learning to underprivileged children — because a good start in school shapes a good start in life.

Empowerment

Project Udaan उड़ान

Equipping women with skills and resources for financial independence — so they don't just survive, they lead.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev जीव

Rescue, protection, and daily feeding for street and injured animals — compassion that extends beyond our own species.

Environment

Project Prakriti प्रकृति

Tree plantations and sustainability drives that work to restore the green cover our communities depend on.

Skilling

Project Vikas विकास

Internships and training that have built digital and professional skills for over 30,000 young people across India.
