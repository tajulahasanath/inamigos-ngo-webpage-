Who we are

Built on the ground, not just on paper.

At a glance

  • Founded: 23 Sept 2020
  • Founder: Mr. Govind Shukla
  • Structure: Section 8 Non-Profit
  • Base: Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh
  • Reach: 28 states

In a world that often feels divided, InAmigos Foundation works toward unity and dignity for every community it touches. What began as a small, local effort in Chhattisgarh has grown into a network of volunteers running programmes across education, nutrition, women's livelihoods, animal welfare, environmental care, and youth skilling.

We don't chase scale for its own sake — every initiative closes a specific gap. That's the InAmigos way — programmatic, local, and accountable.
